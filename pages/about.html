Our Story

Discover the passion and dedication behind MR.COFFEE, where every cup tells a story of quality, innovation, and community.

Susanto Liman

Founder & Master Barista

With over 15 years of experience in the coffee industry, Susanto Liman founded MR.COFFEE with a vision to create a space where coffee lovers can experience the perfect blend of tradition and innovation.

His journey began in the highlands of Indonesia, where he learned the art of coffee cultivation and roasting from local farmers. This deep connection with coffee's origins continues to inspire our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Our Mission

To provide an exceptional coffee experience that brings people together, while maintaining the highest standards of quality and sustainability in every cup we serve.

Our Vision

To be the leading coffee destination that inspires and nurtures a community of coffee enthusiasts, while making a positive impact on the environment and society.

Our Journey

MR.COFFEE began its journey in 2010 with a small coffee shop in the heart of the city. What started as a passion project has grown into a beloved destination for coffee enthusiasts.

Today, we continue to expand our reach while staying true to our roots, serving carefully crafted coffee and creating memorable experiences for our customers.
